Physics Simulations by Gabriel Damon

Bouncing Ball

A ball bouncing with loss of height and speed due to friction. Click in frame and use arrow keys to apply forces to the ball.

1D Collision

Two objects colliding on a frictionless surface. The lighter one is half the weight of the darker one. The collisions are perfectly elastic. Click to add a new block.

Uniform Circular Motion

A ball rotating around a point under the influence of tension and gravity. When a key or the mouse is pressed, the string is cut, sending the rotating ball flying.

Teeter Totter

A block balanced on a teeter-totter. Use the arrow keys to move the block.
